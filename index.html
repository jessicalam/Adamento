About

Andamento Co. is a unique apparel company that specializes in making fresh, sleek, and fashion forward clothes that won’t interfere with your everyday life. Whether it’s our patented 4-way stretch fabric, or our water resistant shoes, our apparel will enhance your life by staying out of the way while keeping you sleek. We deal exclusively to MSU students and faculty by only allowing clients that are on MSU’s IP domain to accesses our store. In order to provide our customers privacy and security of their financial transactions we offer vigorous security and login procedures. Our IaaS and PaaS will be supported by the Microsoft Azure cloud based solution.
